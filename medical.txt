https://www.cms.gov/OpenPayments.  This dataset makes financial relationships between physicians and medical companies more transparent. You can extract the total dollar value of payments made to individual physicians.

https://open.fda.gov/tools/downloads/.  The FDA has posted many datasets here, regarding food recalls, adverse reactions to drugs, and approved medical devices.

https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/u-s-chronic-disease-indicators-cdi.   CDC's medical datasets for chronic disease disorder 
indicators. There are 124 indicators collected from variety of states and cities.

https://www.statista.com/statistics/187221/consumer-price-index-for-medical-care-and-food-in-the-us-since-1960/
Consumer price index for medical care compared to food in the U.S. from 1960 to 2022
